Springfield International School

Welcome to Springfield International School

At Springfield International School, we strive to provide a world-class education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, and social development of our students. Our campus is equipped with modern facilities and state-of-the-art technology, and we offer a rich curriculum designed to prepare students for success in the global arena.

We take pride in our diverse and inclusive environment, where every student is encouraged to reach their full potential. Our experienced and dedicated staff are committed to fostering a supportive and dynamic learning atmosphere.

Join us on this exciting educational journey, and together, we will shape the leaders of tomorrow.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the school hours?

School hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

What is the school’s admission process?

The admission process involves filling out an application form, submitting required documents, and attending an interview.

Are there extracurricular activities?

Yes, we offer a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports, music, arts, and clubs.

What are the fees for the current academic year?

Fees vary depending on the curriculum selected. Please refer to the Tuition Fees section for more details.

How do I contact the school?

You can contact the school via email, phone, or by visiting our contact page on the website.

Is transportation provided for students?

Yes, we provide school buses for transportation within certain areas. Please refer to the transportation section for more details.

What is the teacher-student ratio?

Our average teacher-student ratio is 1:12, ensuring individual attention for each student.

Are meals provided at the school?

Yes, we provide nutritious meals at the school canteen, with both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options.

What is the school's policy on uniforms?

Students are required to wear the school uniform, which can be purchased from the official supplier.

Does the school offer special education services?

Yes, we have a dedicated team to support students with special educational needs and provide the necessary accommodations.
